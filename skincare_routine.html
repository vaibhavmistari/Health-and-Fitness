Skincare Routine

Weeks 1–4 · Barrier reset, then introduce retinol

Goal: Reset your barrier. Same simple routine every day — no actives yet. AM is just water, no foam cleanser.
Goal: Introduce retinol slowly — Monday and Thursday only. Eye serum on 3 non-retinol nights. Wednesday and Friday are rest nights. If anything stings or flakes for more than 2 days, drop a night and double up on moisturizer.

As needed · Any week

🩹 Neutrogena Hydrocolloid Patches: stick on any whitehead overnight. Don't pop — let the patch do the work.

☀️ Sunscreen reapplication: reapply mid-day if outdoors. Especially important on retinol days — UV makes dark marks worse.
